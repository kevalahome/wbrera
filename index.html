⚖️ What is RERA?

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 protects homebuyers by requiring developers to register projects and maintain transparency.

📋 Buyer Safety Checklist

10 things to verify before paying any advance

✅
RERA Registration is Active
Verify on the official WB RERA portal.
📅
Completion Date is in the Future
Check the promised possession date.
💳
70% Funds in Escrow
Under RERA, 70% of buyer funds must be in a designated account.
📜
Land Title is Clear
Ask for an encumbrance certificate.
🏗️
Building Plan Approved
Ensure relevant authority has approved the plan.
📖
Read the Sale Agreement
Check for delay penalty clauses and refund conditions.
🏦
Project is Bank-Approved
If major banks offer loans here, due diligence is done.
⚠️
Check RERA Complaints
Search the RERA portal for complaints against this developer.
🔌
Utility Approvals in Place
Confirm water, electricity, and sewerage approvals.
🤝
Developer Track Record
Research previous projects and speak to residents.
