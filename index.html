📋 Buyer Safety Checklist

10 things to verify before paying any advance on a property

✅
RERA Registration is Active
Verify the project on the official WB RERA portal. An active registration means the developer is legally accountable.
📅
Completion Date is in the Future
Check the promised possession date. If it has already passed, ask for a revised written timeline.
💳
70% Funds in Escrow Account
Under RERA, 70% of buyer payments must be deposited in a separate bank account used only for this project.
📜
Land Title is Clear
Ask for an encumbrance certificate. Confirm there are no legal disputes on the land.
🏗️
Building Plan is Approved
Ensure KMDA, municipal corporation, or relevant authority has sanctioned the building plan.
📖
Read the Sale Agreement Carefully
Check every clause — especially delay penalty, refund terms, and possession conditions before signing.
🏦
Project is Bank-Approved
If SBI, HDFC, or ICICI are offering home loans here, it signals that the bank has done due diligence.
⚠️
Check for RERA Complaints
Search the RERA portal for complaints against this developer or project. Multiple complaints = red flag.
🔌
Utility Approvals in Place
Confirm water, electricity, and sewerage connections are approved or in progress.
🤝
Developer Track Record
Research the builder's previous projects. Visit a completed project and speak to residents.
